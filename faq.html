Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our logistics services

General Questions

What services does TransLogix offer?

TransLogix offers a comprehensive range of logistics services including air freight, sea freight, road transport, and warehousing solutions. We provide end-to-end supply chain management for businesses of all sizes.

How can I get a quote for your services?

You can get an instant quote by visiting our booking page and using our quote calculator. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team directly for personalized quotes.

Which areas do you service?

We operate across major cities in India and provide international shipping services. Check our cities page for detailed information about our service locations.

Shipping & Tracking

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment using the tracking number provided to you at the time of booking. Simply enter the tracking number on our tracking page to get real-time updates.

What is the estimated delivery time?

Delivery times vary depending on the service type and destination. Air freight typically takes 1-3 days, sea freight 15-45 days, and road transport 1-7 days within India.

What items are prohibited for shipping?

Prohibited items include hazardous materials, illegal substances, perishable goods without proper packaging, and items restricted by law. Contact us for a complete list.

Pricing & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and digital payment methods. Corporate clients can also opt for credit terms subject to approval.

Are there any hidden charges?

No, we believe in transparent pricing. All applicable charges will be clearly mentioned in your quote. Additional charges may apply for special handling or services.

Do you offer insurance for shipments?

Yes, we offer comprehensive insurance coverage for all shipments. The cost varies based on the declared value and type of goods.

Customer Support

How can I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7. You can reach us through our contact page, email, phone, or live chat on our website.

What should I do if my shipment is delayed?

Contact our customer support team immediately with your tracking number. We will investigate the cause and provide updates on your shipment status.

Do you have a claims process?

Yes, we have a structured claims process. Contact our customer support within 24 hours of delivery for any issues, and we'll guide you through the process.
